School Achievements

Gold for School with the Best All-Round Facilities in the Singapore Education Awards 2022

Won Gold for Best school with all round facilites

Nexus International School (Singapore) walks the talk when prioritising its learners. The school’s campus was designed to be flexible and open to enhance its learners’ educational experiences. This is why Nexus was awarded Gold for School with the Best All-Round Facilities in the Singapore Education Awards 2022! Additionally, Nexus's innovative learning environment and goal of cultivating future-ready students have clinched the school with the silver awards for Best Innovative Learning Environment and Entrepreneurship in Education. Such outstanding achievements, we say!
